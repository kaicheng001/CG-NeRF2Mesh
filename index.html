Abstract

This project page presents the experimental results of the final assignment for the Computer Graphics course in the fall semester of the 2024-2025 academic year. The project utilizes the Nerf2Mesh framework and the Nerf Synthetic dataset to reconstruct and visualize two scenes: Lego and Ship.

The following sections display the results for the Lego and Ship scenes. Each group of videos includes four segments: the zero-stage RGB video, the zero-stage depth video, the first-stage RGB video, and the first-stage depth video.
